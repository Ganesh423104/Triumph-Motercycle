LIFESTYLE COLLECTION

Introducing Triumph Motorcycles Lifestyle collection. Combining a passion for the finest fabrics and our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship, we have reimagined authentic icons for a contemporary moto-influenced style. MOTORCYCLE WEAR

Our motorcycle range is designed for riders by riders. Why? Because they know better than anyone the importance of having the right kit. We've spent years crafting our CE-approved riderwear, perfecting the delicate balance of authentic style, innovative design and cutting-edge technology. The result is a timeless collection made for the ride.

CASUAL WEAR

We've a reputation for creating classics, and our casual wear collection is no exception. Designed in-house, our range features modern clothing executed with craftsmanship and care. Jackets and sweaters sit alongside graphic print T-shirts and polos to give you authentic style that stands out from the crowd.

TECHNOLOGY

D3O®

"Naturally we take rider safety very seriously, which is why we’ve partnered with D3O® to incorporate this high performance CE-certified impact protection into so many of our own exclusive motorcycle clothing designs. D3O® technology performs to the highest of industry standards and is suitable for all riders. It also enables Triumph to create a wide range of stylish gear without compromising comfort and flexibility."

GORE-TEX®

"As part of our obsessive desire to help every rider get equipped and prepared to take on any adventure with confidence whatever the riding conditions, our adverse weather textile riderwear utilises Gore-Tex® technology. Designed to be light, breathable and weatherproof, the Gore-Tex® fabrics used in Triumph clothing are rigorously tested for maximum protection from the elements while still offering comfort and style."
